Will Knapp trying not to trip at Kings Canyon NP. Photo Credit: Natalie Hunter

Hi! I'm Will and I like to think of myself as a Jack of Creative Trades. Anywhere I can eek out an ability to create something artful, I take. It inspired my to take up many trades from live theater (set/prop building and sound), to event, nature and street photography, to a degree in computer science. I've always been inspired to create art and my favorite part of most art is capturing live moments

I'v been a hobbyist photographer since 2014, and more recently have had the pleasure of working with some amazing individuals and groups to capture various events and milestones. Most recently I was honored to be asked to work with other photogaphers to capture the Citi Bank Pride Parade showing. Beyond that I love challenging myself to see that beauty in every day moments which is why I love street and nature photography so much. Tiny everyday moments memorialized in time can capture so much raw emotion.

I've also had the pleasure of working with bands and shows to capture live performances, or at least rehearsals of performances to use for publicity. Capturing live events like this is so fun and presents it's own challenges such as capturing a band without making the show less enjoyable for others to anticipating big moments of a stage production and being properly positioned without being too swept up in the art. I relish the challenges though as each event shoot brings its own flavor of fun and challenge.

If you would like me to capture a moment for you, or are looking for stock photos of nature or street photography feel free to contact me! I'll get back to you right away with any info you want.
